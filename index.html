'The fans deserve a semi-final'

Canary Islander views Tuesday's second leg against PSG as one of the most important matches of his career so far

Pedri marked his return from injury by providing a fantastic assist for Raphinha to score the equaliser at Parc dels Princes six days ago, and now he is looking forward to the return leg against PSG knowing that "the fans deserve a semi-final. We have been hoping for too long to give them nights like this.”
Barça have a 3-2 lead and home advantage, but even so, the Canary Islander insists "that doesn't guarantee anything. But I know that the fans will be behind us from the moment we take to the field and we'll give everything we have got."
